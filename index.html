⚠️ News Trading Rules

🚫
Don't Trade 30min Before/After
Spreads widen, slippage kills. Wait for the dust to settle.
📉
Reduce Position Size by 50%
If you must trade, cut risk. Volatility can be 3x normal.
✅
Trade the Technical Setup After
Wait for price to settle into a clean structure, then enter.
🎯
Use Wider Stops During News
Normal 20-pip SL won't survive NFP. Plan for 50-100 pips.
